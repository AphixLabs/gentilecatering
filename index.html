Bobby Gentile is part of a long history in the event industry. He has always valued the trust of his customers and the kindness of the communities he has become a part of. In this latest evolution, responsible environmental practices, support of homegrown businesses and an eye toward charitable endeavours will become business as usual.

Bobby Gentile fait partie d'une longue histoire dans l'industrie événementielle. Il a toujours valorisé la confiance de ses clients et la bienveillance des communautés dont il fait partie. Pratiques environnementales responsables, soutien aux entreprises locales et initiatives caritatives — voilà notre quotidien.
